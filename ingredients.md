The ingredients that are used in cooking Palak paneer are-
Butter-1 Tbsp
Paneer-200 gms
Coriander-5 gms
Tomato- 2 medium size tomatoes
Garam masala-1 tea spoon
Palak - one bunch
Cream-1 Tbsp
Onion-2 medium size
Cashew-4 to 5
Garlic-4 to 5
Cumin-1 Tsp
Ginger-2 to 3 inch
Green chili-3
Salt-Acording to the taste
Chili powder-1Tsp
Haldi powder-1Tsp
